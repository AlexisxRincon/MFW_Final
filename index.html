About Me

I was born in Fresno, CA but moved down south to San Diego before I even turned one. Ever since I could remember i've always had an interest in creative feilds. It wasn't till my last year of high school where i really started expolring my creativity. It first started with filming and editing, then I got into Graphic Design, then Web Design, and now I'm here very much interested in Web Development. My main goal in life has always been to start a clothing brand. It wasn't till the Websites for beginners course than I saw that option as a posibility because of the help and support from my instructors and classmates. I have now started activly working on it and am exiceted to continue this journey.
